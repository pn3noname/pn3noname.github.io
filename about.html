Why This Blog?

This blog exists for several reasons. First, it's a personal knowledge repository where I can reference my own notes and writeups. Second, it's a way to give back to the cybersecurity community that has provided so many resources for my learning. Third, it serves as a portfolio demonstrating my commitment to continuous learning and improvement.

I believe that teaching is one of the best ways to learn. By documenting my learning process and sharing what I discover, I solidify my own understanding while potentially helping others who are on similar paths.
